The Problem

The W-2 playbook runs out fast.

You have clients earning $200K–$600K+ in W-2 income. You've already walked them through every standard play. The options after that are limited — and they know it.

  • ✕ Max the 401K — they're doing it. Still paying a six-figure tax bill.
  • ✕ Backdoor Roth — appreciated, but it moves the needle by thousands, not hundreds of thousands.
  • ✕ HSA, FSA, charitable giving — valuable, but not what a $400K earner is asking about in March.
  • ✕ "Let me look into some options" — they'll find someone else who already has them.
The STR loophole is the real answer. Most CPAs just can't execute it.

A properly structured short-term rental — with documented material participation — can generate $80K–$150K+ in paper losses that offset W-2 income directly. Not deferred. Not someday. In year one.

You already know the strategy. The gap isn't knowledge — it's execution. Who sources the property, manages the build, furnishes it, operates it, and tracks the hours?

That's what Rise does. You stay in your lane. We handle everything else — and your client never forgets you're the one who made it happen.
